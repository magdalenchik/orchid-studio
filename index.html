About us

Orchid Studio is a professional dance studio set up to provide professional training in many styles of dance, while having the space to develop the individual's career to their utmost potential. It focuses on building a dancer's mindset by providing small classes that allow students to work with teachers who have dedicated their lives to the art of dance. They are dedicated to teaching the children who come to them, the passion of dance and how to channel their emotion in a way that will help them overcome the physical and emotional obstacles to them.
